Privacy Policy - Scholarship Finder

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Scholarship Finder, an application designed to help students discover, filter, and track eligible scholarships based on their personal, academic, and financial details. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy outlines:

  • What data we collect and how we use it.
  • How we store and secure your data.
  • Your rights regarding your data.
  • How to contact us for concerns or inquiries.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal & Academic Information

When you register or use the app, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Age and gender
  • State and location (for region-based scholarships)
  • Education level, board/university, and academic performance
  • Category (General / SC / ST / OBC / EWS)
  • Family income range and parent occupation

1.2 Usage Data

  • Device and app usage information
  • IP address and basic device identifiers
  • App activity timestamps
  • Error logs and performance analytics

1.3 External Links

Scholarship Finder provides links to official scholarship websites (government and private). We do not control these external sites and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Match you with eligible scholarships
  • Display personalized scholarship recommendations
  • Enable saving and tracking of scholarships
  • Improve filtering accuracy and user experience
  • Send reminders for deadlines and updates
  • Enhance app performance and reliability

3. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure server communication (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to sensitive information
  • Regular monitoring and updates

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and update your profile information
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Government scholarship portals
  • Official education and scholarship websites
  • Analytics tools for app improvement

6. Children's Privacy

Scholarship Finder is intended for students. Users below 13 years should use the app only with parental or guardian consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us:

  • Email: support@scholarshipfinder.app
  • App: Use the "Contact Us" option inside the app.
